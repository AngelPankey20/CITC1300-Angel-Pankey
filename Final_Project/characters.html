Don't Starve Together: Characters

Wilson Percival Higgsbury

The Gentleman Scientist

Wilson, the first character available, is known for his ability to grow a magnificent beard, which provides insulation during winter. He's a balanced and beginner-friendly choice.

Willow

The Firestarter

Willow is immune to fire damage and comes with her trusty lighter, Bernie the teddy bear, and the ability to thrive near flames, making her perfect for managing sanity in the dark.

Wolfgang

The Strongman

Wolfgang grows stronger when well-fed but becomes weaker when his hunger drops. He's a powerhouse in combat, ideal for players who love to fight.

Wendy

The Bereaved

Wendy summons her ghostly twin sister, Abigail, to help fight enemies. She's great for crowd control but suffers from reduced damage output.

WX-78

The Soulless Automaton

WX-78 can upgrade itself using gears and is immune to food spoilage. However, it takes damage from rain, making weather management crucial.

Wickerbottom

The Librarian

Wickerbottom is a knowledgeable character with access to unique crafting recipes and books that can summon effects, but she suffers from a strict sleep schedule.

Woodie

The Lumberjack

Woodie has a deep connection to trees and transforms into a werebeaver, weremoose, or weregoose depending on the situation, offering versatile gameplay options.

Wes

The Silent

Wes is a challenge character with reduced stats, making survival more difficult. He's great for experienced players looking for a tougher experience.

Maxwell

The Puppet Master

Maxwell can summon shadow puppets to assist in tasks, balancing sanity costs for efficiency. His fragile health makes him challenging yet rewarding.

Wigfrid

The Battleborn

Wigfrid excels in combat and gains health and sanity from fighting, but she can only eat meat, requiring strategic food management.

Webber

The Spiderboy

Webber can befriend spiders and has unique interactions with them, offering a fresh perspective on gameplay.
